Who we are

Nivienka is a friendly community centre that hosts creative workshops, family-friendly events and seasonal programs. Our mission is to create an inclusive space where everyone feels welcome.

What we offer

  • Creative workshops and crafts
  • Family activities and play
  • Community meals and potlucks
  • Seasonal celebrations
